Measures of Dispersion

Measures of dispersion describe the extent to which data values are spread or scattered around a central value. While measures of central tendency indicate the average behavior, dispersion measures help assess variability, consistency, and reliability of data.

In engineering, dispersion is crucial for performance analysis, quality control, reliability testing, and data-driven modeling.
