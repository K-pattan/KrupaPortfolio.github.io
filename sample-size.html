Sample Size Determination

Sample size determination is the process of selecting an appropriate number of observations or subjects from a population to ensure reliable and accurate statistical results.

Choosing the correct sample size is essential because:

  • Too small a sample gives unreliable results
  • Too large a sample increases cost and time
  • Correct sample size balances accuracy and efficiency

Factors Affecting Sample Size

  • Confidence Level: Higher confidence ‚Üí larger sample
  • Margin of Error: Smaller error ‚Üí larger sample
  • Population Variability: More variability ‚Üí larger sample
  • Population Size: Important for small populations
