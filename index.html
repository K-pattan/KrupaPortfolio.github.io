Introduction to the Course

Probability and Statistics form the mathematical foundation for analyzing data and dealing with uncertainty. This course introduces methods to collect, summarize, and interpret data using descriptive statistics and visual tools. Statistical inference techniques such as estimation and hypothesis testing help in making reliable decisions.
