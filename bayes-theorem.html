Bayes‚Äô Theorem is a fundamental result in probability theory that provides a method for updating the probability of an event based on new information. It relates conditional probabilities of events and allows reverse inference.

This theorem forms the backbone of Bayesian statistics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and decision-making systems.

Statement of Bayes‚Äô Theorem

P(A | B) = [ P(B | A) √ó P(A) ] / P(B)

This formula allows us to compute the probability of a cause (A) given the observed effect (B).
